Foundry

Foundry is the front door to our work. It is built for platform, security, and delivery teams who want tighter control without heavier process.

Clear artifacts

Foundry makes it easy to produce “what happened” output that travels with the release. When questions come later, the answer is already packaged.

Diagnostics-first

Problems surface as actionable failures with a fast path to remediation. Teams spend less time guessing and more time fixing.

Built to scale

Start simple. Expand coverage across repos and services without rebuilding your workflow. The tool grows with the organization.
